ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Kelvin, a student from Ngee Ann Polytechnic. I have always been fascinated by video games, especially MOBA games like League of Legends. I have spent countless hours playing and exploring the different aspects of these games, from the intricate gameplay mechanics to the immersive storylines. As I grew more familiar with the gaming industry, I became increasingly interested in the technical side of game development. I started reading about game programming and learning how games are designed and built. The idea of creating something that people could enjoy and engage with as much as I have always excited me. That's why I decided to pursue a career in game programming, so I could use my passion for gaming to build something meaningful and impactful for others to enjoy.
